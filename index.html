How to Play Flytora – Typing Game Instructions

  1. Open Flytora in your browser — no download or registration needed.
  2. Choose your difficulty: Easy, Medium, Hard, or Insane.
  3. Choose your game mode: Classic (survive as long as possible), Sprint (race against time), or Survival (limited lives).

  5. Type the word shown on screen as fast and accurately as possible.
  6. Each correct word makes your bird flap and fly through the gap in the wall.
  7. The faster you type, the higher your score multiplier and WPM count.
  8. Collect power-ups (Shield, Freeze, Slow-Mo, Double Points) during gameplay.
  9. Earn coins to unlock new bird skins and trails in the Shop.
  10. Beat your high score and compete on the global leaderboard!

Tips to Type Faster – How to Improve Typing Speed

  • Use all 10 fingers: Touch typing (using all fingers on the home row) is the fastest method. Practice placing fingers on ASDF and JKL; without looking.
  • Don't look at the keyboard: Training yourself to type without looking forces your fingers to develop muscle memory.
  • Focus on accuracy first: Speed naturally increases once your fingers know where the keys are. Don't rush — type correctly first.
  • Practice daily for 15-20 minutes: Consistent short practice beats occasional long sessions. Play Flytora daily for the fastest improvement.
  • Start on Easy mode: Build confidence and accuracy on Easy before moving to harder difficulties.
  • Use the combo multiplier: Stringing correct words together builds your combo multiplier, giving you more points and helping you track your consistency.
  • Don't rush — let the rhythm come: Good typists type in a steady rhythm. Rushing leads to more errors and actually slows you down.
